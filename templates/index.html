About Us

Plenus is designed to bridge the gap between those in need of medical assistance and medical practitioners. Since in the traditional setting its somewhat difficult to find a therapist, especially for those people suffering from depression and have suicidal thoughts, Plenus brings the help to them.

  • Personalized support

Therapists

Individual

Individuals and their mental health are deeply interconnected. How individuals manage their mental well-being, including practicing self-care and seeking support when needed, can significantly impact their overall quality of life and emotional resilience

Couples

The quality of couples' mental health significantly impacts their relationship, as conflicts and mental health challenges can strain trust and cooperation within partnerships. Strong mental well-being within couples often contributes to more harmonious and trusting relationships

Psychiatrist

Mental health professionals play a vital role in treating mental health disorders and improving individuals' well-being, offering crucial support and guidance in managing mental health challenges. Their expertise helps reduce the stigma associated with mental health issues and enhances overall mental health in society

Family and friends

Family and friends play a pivotal role in supporting mental health by providing emotional support and creating a sense of belonging, reducing the risk of mental health issues, and contributing to overall well-being Their presence fosters social connectedness, lowers anxiety and depression rates, and enhances self-esteem

Online Therapist

Online therapists provide the convenience of virtual counseling, making mental health support accessible from anywhere. They offer a range of therapeutic approaches to suit individual needs

Clinical Social Worker

A peer counselor is an individual with adequate experience about mental health challenges who provides support, understanding, and guidance to others facing similar issues, playing a crucial role in fostering emotional well-being and reducing stigma associated with mental health

Significance of mental health.

When a person is mentally healthy, just like physical health, they are able to:

  • Experience positive emotions
  • Cope with callenges better
  • Have a healthy and functional body .
  • Maintain good relationships .
  • Increased productivity .
  • Overall happiness and satisfaction in both their lives and other people lives.

Impacts of mentall illness

Those who experience mental illnesses experience a darker sort of reality

A weakened immune system and increased risk of cardiovascular diseases. Broken and unstable relationships. Unable to focus on tasks or solve problemss. Low resilience and increased stigma. An unsatisfactory life. Low productivity, negative economic growth and lack of social cohesion in society.
